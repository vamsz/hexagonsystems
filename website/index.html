Platform 01

Sentinel CCTV AI

Autonomous Urban Surveillance & Threat Prevention

Sentinel plugs into existing IP camera networks and watches every feed 24/7. No new hardware needed. It detects threats in real time, tracks suspects across cameras, and turns passive recording into active prevention.

  • Behavioral Threat Alerts — Detects fights, running mobs, shoplifting, vandalism instantly
  • Cross-Camera Suspect Search — Upload a mugshot, trace them across every camera in the city
  • Abandoned Object Detection — Flags unattended luggage at transit hubs automatically
  • Virtual Fences & Intrusion — Invisible boundaries that trigger instant patrol dispatch
  • Autonomous Command Center — 1 AI node monitors 500+ feeds with zero fatigue

Platform 02

IntelTrace

AI-Powered Investigation Intelligence Platform

Every case generates hundreds of entities. IntelTrace reads every document like a trained analyst, builds a growing intelligence graph, and automatically surfaces cross-case connections that investigators would never find manually.

  • AI Case Analyst — Upload FIRs, forensic reports, witness statements; AI summarises and identifies suspects
  • CDR Intelligence — Maps caller networks, flags frequent contacts, highlights suspicious patterns
  • Investigation Graph — Every entity plotted on a live, interactive map of relationships
  • Cross-Case Discovery — Same phone or vehicle in multiple cases? Flagged instantly across districts
  • Role-Based Access — Officers see their cases. Supervisors see district-level. State-level for leadership

Process

How It Works

From raw data to actionable intelligence in four steps

01

Connect

Plug into existing cameras or upload case documents — FIRs, CDRs, forensic reports, bank statements. Any format.

02

Analyse

AI reads every document and feed like a trained analyst. Identifies persons, phones, vehicles, locations, and behaviours.

03

Map

Every entity becomes a node. Every relationship becomes a link. The intelligence graph grows with every case and camera.

04

Discover

Cross-references every prior case and feed. Surfaces hidden connections across districts, years, and jurisdictions automatically.

Capabilities

Platform Capabilities

Deep-dive into what each platform delivers

Behavioral Threat Alerts

Proactively detects fights, running mobs, shoplifting, and vandalism without anyone calling. Real-time alerts to command center.

Cross-Camera Suspect Search

Upload a photo. The system searches weeks of footage across all cameras to map everywhere that suspect has been.

Abandoned Object Detection

Automatically flags unattended luggage left at transit hubs or government buildings longer than a safe threshold.

Intrusion & Virtual Fences

Draw invisible boundaries on any camera feed. Anyone crossing after hours triggers instant patrol dispatch.

IntelTrace Integration

When crime is detected on camera, Sentinel automatically sends the evidence clip and tracking data into IntelTrace.

Command Center

Transforms passive recording rooms into active prevention hubs. 1 AI node replaces what would take dozens of human operators.

AI Case Analyst

Upload FIRs, forensic reports, witness statements. The AI summarises each case, identifies suspects, and assigns probability scores.

CDR Intelligence

Upload call records. Maps caller networks, flags frequent contacts, and highlights suspicious communication patterns.

Investigation Graph

Every entity (person, phone, vehicle, location) plotted on a live, interactive map of relationships. Click any node to see all connections.

Cross-Case Detection

When the same phone, person, or vehicle appears in multiple cases, the system flags it instantly — even across districts.

Role-Based Access

Officers see only their cases. Supervisors see district-level data. State-level view available for senior leadership.

Network Effect

More cases = smarter system. At 500+ cases, the graph becomes a crime intelligence engine surfacing organised networks and syndicates.
